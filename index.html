Why this exists

AI is moving faster than your reading list.

If you lead a business function, a practice, or an enterprise, you already know the ground is shifting. Agentic AI isn't a research curiosity anymore; it's showing up in procurement decks, board presentations, and client RFPs. The question isn't whether AI will transform your industry. It's whether you'll be the one leading the transformation or reacting to it.

The problem is that keeping up is nearly impossible. By the time a development makes it into a strategy journal or a Big Four whitepaper, the landscape has moved again. And most of what's written about AI is still aimed at engineers, not the people making investment decisions.

That's the gap Ambient Advantage fills. Every weekday morning, I distill the most consequential AI developments into what they actually mean for your business: the strategic implications, the competitive risks, and the moves worth making. No jargon, no hype cycle cheerleading, just the signal you need to lead with confidence.

This isn't another tech newsletter. It's an intelligence brief for leaders who are accountable for outcomes.
